# Carrier Matrix

## How to Use This File

This file is the primary reference for carrier appetite and capabilities. When selecting markets for a new submission, read this file to identify which carriers are likely to quote a given risk.

**Decision process:**
1. Identify the line of business needed.
2. Filter carriers that write that line.
3. Check geographic and risk characteristic restrictions.
4. Check premium range fit.
5. Rank by appetite match and submission method efficiency.
6. Cross-reference `carriers/submission_preferences.md` for formatting requirements before submitting.
7. **When two carriers have equal appetite, route to the one earning more PMSF** (see PMSF table below).

**Maintenance:** Update this file whenever carrier appetite changes — after underwriter calls, appetite guide updates, or lost quotes that reveal new restrictions. See `carriers/market_notes/` for the logging convention.

## PMSF Compensation Reference (MIAA / SIAA 2026)

*Source: MIAA 2026 PMSF Report Card — confidential, internal Anvo use only. For full tier breakdowns, year-end calculations, and lock-in provisions, see MIAA PMSF 2026 Intercom article (ID 14465386).*

PMSF (Portfolio Management Service Fee) is an override commission paid by carriers to SIAA member agencies on top of normal agent commission. Anvo earns PMSF through its MIAA membership (MIAA is the regional SIAA master agency for the Midwest). PMSF compounds with premium volume — it pays on every dollar placed with participating carriers. **When two carriers have equal appetite for an account, route to the one earning more PMSF.**

### Three-Layer PMSF Structure

1. **SIAA National Quarterly Fee** — Flat % of submitted premium, paid quarterly. No volume threshold — Anvo earns from dollar one.
2. **SIAA National Year-End Partnering Fee** — Paid annually after year-end. Requires hitting national volume thresholds per carrier.
3. **MIAA Local Deals** — Negotiated directly by MIAA with specific carriers. Tiered: Tier 1 = guaranteed year-end bonus; Tier 5 = volume threshold Anvo rarely reaches. Paid by MIAA on top of SIAA amounts.
