# Carrier Submission Preferences

## How to Use This File

Before submitting a risk to any carrier, check this file to confirm exactly what they require. Submitting incomplete or incorrectly formatted packages causes delays and damages relationships with underwriters.

**Rule:** Never submit to a carrier without first verifying their current requirements here. If a carrier is not listed, check their portal or contact the underwriter before submitting — then add them to this file.

## Quick Reference: Universal Minimums

Regardless of carrier, every submission should include at minimum:

1. ACORD 125 (Commercial Insurance Application) — fully completed, no blank fields
2. ACORD form specific to the line of business (126 for GL, 130 for WC, 137 for Auto, etc.)
3. Currently valued loss runs — minimum 3 years unless carrier specifies more
4. Description of operations in the insured's own words or from their website
5. Cover letter summarizing the risk and what you're looking for (see `templates/` for cover letter templates)
