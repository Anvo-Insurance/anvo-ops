## Liberty Mutual Insurance — Small Commercial

**AM Best Rating:** NOT SPECIFIED IN GUIDE — verify with UW
**Carrier type:** Standard
**Primary lines offered:** BOP, Package, Commercial Auto, Workers Compensation, Umbrella
**Geographic availability:** Nationwide — *target classes noted as countrywide; some exclusions apply (see CA, FL, GA, NJ, NY notation on appetite guide pages — verify per class with UW)*

---

### Target Market

**Business size sweet spot:** Small commercial (see Eligibility Parameters table below for industry-specific thresholds). Accounts exceeding thresholds may be routed to middle market with ACORD application submitted to state mailbox.
**New ventures:** Limited — guide consistently lists "more than three years in business" as a preferred characteristic across all industries. New ventures not explicitly excluded but disfavored.

### Industry Appetite — Classes Written

- Fine dining (high-quality food, formal setting, table service, upscale ambience)
- Casual family dining, full service (informal setting, table service, average to low price point)
- Quick service (no table service; limited cooking — electric grills, toasters, warming/roller ovens, warming trays, microwave, baking, non-wood-fired pizza ovens; NO grease-laden vapors)
- Fast casual (freshly prepared, hand-made, no table service; full cooking allowed including frying, deep fat frying, grilling, broiling, barbecuing — grease-laden vapors OK)

### Hard Declines — Will NOT Write

**Cross-industry declines (appear across multiple segments):**
- Cannabis / marijuana / CBD in any form (retail, manufacturing, distribution, consulting)
- Tobacco products and shops
- 24-hour operations (retail, commercial RE, hospitality)
- Bars, nightclubs, taverns, adult entertainment and lounges (standalone or as primary tenant)
- Check-cashing and pawn shops
- Firearms, aircraft, watercraft, railroad/locomotive products or services
- Pharmaceuticals and nutraceuticals (manufacturing, distribution, consulting)
- Medical equipment and invasive medical products
- Environmental/hazardous remediation (mold, lead, asbestos, water, fire damage)
- Salvage operations
- Employee/labor leasing, temporary staffing, labor unions

**Manufacturing-specific declines:**
- Tobacco, pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals, chemicals, energy drinks, medicinal ingredients, dietary/health supplements
- Firearms, aircraft, watercraft, medical equipment, invasive medical products, railroad/locomotive
- Oysters or sushi-grade seafood, oyster beds/farms, hatcheries
- Grain milling, baby food, dextrin, extracts, feed, liquor, packing houses, sugar refining, syrup/molasses refining, rendering works, spices

**Wholesale/Distribution-specific declines:**
- Tobacco, cannabis including CBD, pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals
- Firearms, aircraft, watercraft, railroad/locomotive, contractors' equipment products
- Fertilizers, chemicals, fuel
- Liquor, baby food, dextrine, extract, spice; grain milling or feed; packing houses; sugar/syrup/molasses refining; rendering works
- Livestock and hatcheries (farm risks in applicable states only)
- Salvage, oyster/sushi-grade seafood
- Third-party distribution (distributing on behalf of a third party)
- Backhauling >10% of annual miles for a particular auto or account
- Regular travel beyond 200-mile radius

**Construction-specific declines:**
- Roofing
- Work at height >3 stories
- Underground work >4 feet or requiring trench box
- Crane operations performed by insured (not subbed to third party)
- Work at airports, hospitals, nursing homes (high-risk facilities)
- Blasting, tunneling, high-voltage equipment/lines
- Mold/lead/asbestos/water/fire damage remediation; waterproofing/sealing
- Residential condominiums, multi-unit buildings, tract home developments

**Restaurant-specific declines:**
- Sports bars, taverns, lounges, clubs (high alcohol sales)
- Raw-primary menu operations (oyster bars, sushi restaurants)
- Tableside cooking, buffet-style service
- Standalone food truck operations
- Employee food delivery

**Professional services declines:**
- Consulting in: environmental, tobacco, pharmaceutical, aircraft, firearms, watercraft, medical, railroad or oil and gas industries
- Businesses whose employees routinely travel outside the immediate area

**Business & Personal Services declines:**
- Med spa services (tanning, hair implantation, face lift, chemical peel, cryotherapy, sauna, body piercing, tattoo, permanent makeup)
- Pet rescue, adoption, sheltering or fostering; rare or exotic pets

### Preferred Risk Characteristics by Line

**Commercial Auto:**
- Experienced drivers without violations
- Well-established business (3+ years)
- No personal use of vehicles
- Best industries: General services, Health services, Manufacturing, Professional services, Real estate

**Package (BOP/Commercial Property):**
- Premises and building in good condition
- Fully occupied location
- 100% insurance to value (ITV)
- Best industries: Finance, Health services, Professional services, Real estate, Wholesale

**Workers Compensation:**
- Accurate payroll and exposures reported
- Credit experience modification
- Low auto exposure
- Best industries: Health services, Information, Professional science and technology, Professional services, Real estate, Restaurants, Retail
