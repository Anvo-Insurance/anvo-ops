# Submission Checklist: Business Owners Policy (BOP)

## Purpose

Use this checklist to verify that all required information is gathered before submitting a BOP risk to carriers. BOPs package general liability and commercial property (and sometimes business income) into a single policy, so both GL and property information are needed.

Cross-reference `carriers/submission_preferences.md` for carrier-specific requirements.

---

## Required ACORD Forms

- [ ] **ACORD 125** — Commercial Insurance Application
- [ ] **ACORD 126** — Commercial General Liability Section
- [ ] **ACORD 140** — Property Section (or equivalent BOP-specific application from the carrier)

**Note:** Most BOP carriers use their own proprietary application rather than standard ACORD forms. Check `carriers/submission_preferences.md` for each carrier's specific app. The ACORD forms listed above are the fallback baseline.

## Business Information

- [ ] **Legal entity name** and DBA (if different)
- [ ] **Entity type** — sole proprietor, LLC, corporation, partnership, nonprofit
- [ ] **FEIN / Tax ID**
- [ ] **Years in business**
- [ ] **Years of management experience** in this type of business
- [ ] **Annual gross revenue**
- [ ] **Number of employees** (full-time, part-time, seasonal)
- [ ] **Description of operations** — detailed, specific, in the insured's own words
- [ ] **SIC / NAICS code** (if known) — or enough description to classify
- [ ] **Website URL** (carriers will look at it — make sure it's accurate and current)

## Location / Property Information

For **each location**:

- [ ] **Street address**
- [ ] **Owned or leased?**
- [ ] **If leased:** landlord name, lease terms, does lease require insurance?
- [ ] **Building construction type** — frame, joisted masonry, non-combustible, masonry non-combustible, modified fire-resistive, fire-resistive
- [ ] **Year built**
- [ ] **Year of last major renovation** (roof, wiring, plumbing, HVAC)
- [ ] **Square footage**
- [ ] **Number of stories**
- [ ] **Occupancy** — what percentage does the insured occupy? Other tenants?
- [ ] **Protection class** (ISO) — if unknown, can be looked up by address
- [ ] **Sprinklered?** Full or partial? NFPA 13 or 13R?
- [ ] **Alarm system?** Central station monitored? Fire, burglar, or both?
- [ ] **Building value** (replacement cost)
- [ ] **Business personal property value** (contents, equipment, inventory)
- [ ] **Business income / extra expense** — estimated annual revenue at this location, maximum period of restoration needed

## Liability Information

- [ ] **GL limits requested** — occurrence / aggregate
- [ ] **Products/completed operations exposure?** Describe.
- [ ] **Liquor liability exposure?** (serve, sell, manufacture alcohol?)
- [ ] **Professional services provided?** (may need separate professional liability)
- [ ] **Any subcontractors used?** Do they carry their own insurance?
- [ ] **Prior GL claims in last 5 years?** Details.

## Loss History

- [ ] **Currently valued loss runs** — minimum 5 years
  - Must include both GL and property claims
  - Dated within 60 days of submission
- [ ] **Large loss detail** — for any claim over $25,000 threshold

## Supplemental Documents

- [ ] **Photos of building** — exterior (all four sides), interior (main areas), signage, roof (if accessible)
- [ ] **Roof inspection report** (if required by carrier — varies by carrier and building age; check `carriers/submission_preferences.md`)
- [ ] **Prior policy declaration page**
- [ ] **Carrier-specific supplemental application** (check `carriers/submission_preferences.md`)

## Common BOP Endorsements to Discuss with Client

- [ ] **Equipment breakdown** (boiler & machinery)
- [ ] **Cyber liability** (sublimit or standalone?)
- [ ] **Employment practices liability** (EPLI — sublimit or standalone?)
- [ ] **Hired & non-owned auto** (if no separate auto policy)
- [ ] **Spoilage** (for restaurants, grocers, etc.)
- [ ] **Ordinance or law coverage** (for older buildings)
- [ ] **Signs coverage** (if exterior signage has significant value)

## Pre-Submission Verification

Before submitting, confirm:

1. All ACORD forms are fully completed.
2. Building construction and year built are confirmed (not estimated).
3. Revenue and property values are current-year figures.
4. Loss runs cover the full period and are currently valued.
5. Photos are included if carrier requires them.
6. Carrier-specific supplementals are attached (see `carriers/submission_preferences.md`).
7. The risk falls within the target carrier's BOP appetite (see `carriers/carrier_matrix.md`) — check class code eligibility, building age limits, and geographic restrictions.
